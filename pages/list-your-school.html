For training schools

Get your school listed on Upshift

Free to list. No paid placements, ever — your position is always earned through rider reviews, never bought.

Why list with us

  • Free listing — we don't charge schools to appear, and we never will charge for a better position in results.
  • Reach riders in Edinburgh actively searching for CBT, A1, A2, Full A and Mod 1 & 2 training.
  • Build a track record of honest reviews over time, rather than relying on paid ads.

What we need from you

Upshift is run by hand right now, not a self-serve dashboard — email us the following and we'll get your profile live or corrected:

  • School name and address
  • Licences you teach (CBT, A1, A2, Full A, Mod 1 & 2)
  • Contact details — phone, email, website
  • Opening hours, if you'd like them shown

Already listed?

A handful of Edinburgh schools are already listed on Upshift, compiled from publicly available information. If that's you and any details need correcting — or you'd rather not be listed — use the same address below and we'll sort it quickly.
