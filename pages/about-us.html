About Upshift

Why we built this, where it is today, and where it's going.

Why Upshift exists

Working out which motorbike licence you need, which schools nearby actually teach it, and what it's going to cost shouldn't take an afternoon of tab-switching between school websites that don't list a price. Upshift exists to put that comparison in one place — pick a licence, see who teaches it near you, and see what it costs, side by side.

Where we are today

We're piloting in Edinburgh with a small number of schools while we get the basics right — search, licence information, and real pricing. There are no rider reviews yet; that's the next thing we're building, not something we're pretending already exists.

Who's behind it

Upshift is run independently, not by a training school or a DVSA-affiliated body. We're not a government service and we don't provide training ourselves — we're a directory that points you to the people who do.

What's next

More UK cities beyond Edinburgh, genuine rider reviews, and — down the line — sponsored placements as a way to fund the site. See how we rank schools for what that will and won't change.
