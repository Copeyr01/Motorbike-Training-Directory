Cookie policy

Short answer: we don't use tracking or advertising cookies today.

What are cookies

Small files a website can store in your browser — often used for logins, remembering preferences, or tracking visits across sites.

What GetOnBikes uses today

GetOnBikes doesn't currently set any tracking, advertising or analytics cookies. Our hosting provider may use minimal technical cookies needed to serve the site — these aren't used to track you or build a profile.

What might change

If we add analytics or other tools that use cookies in future, we'll update this page — and ask for consent where required — before that happens.

Controlling cookies

You can block or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings.

Contact

Questions about this policy: hello@getonbikes.co.uk

This is a plain-English policy written for an early-stage site. It hasn't been reviewed by a solicitor — it should be updated the moment any tracking, analytics or advertising cookie is actually added.
