About

Edinburgh Motorcycle Training is an independent school based in Straiton, on the southern edge of the city, offering Compulsory Basic Training (CBT) and full licence (DAS) courses. Training starts at a dedicated off-road site before moving on to Edinburgh's roads.

Courses offered

CBT Compulsory Basic Training

Runs on weekdays and weekends (weekend and bank holiday courses priced slightly higher). Required before riding on the road on a provisional licence.

Full A Direct Access (DAS)

Full, unrestricted motorcycle licence training.

Course pricing changes — confirm current prices directly with the school.
