About

Saltire Motorcycle Training runs CBT and full licence (DAS) courses from Gorgie Road, with off-road training at their Saughton site before moving on to Edinburgh's roads. Bike hire and protective equipment are included in the course price.

Courses offered

CBT Compulsory Basic Training

Required before riding on the road on a provisional licence. Bike hire and kit included.

Full A Direct Access (DAS)

Full, unrestricted motorcycle licence training, plus advanced rider training.

Course pricing changes — confirm current prices directly with the school.
