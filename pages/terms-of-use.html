Terms of use

The short version: use the site fairly, and don't rely on it instead of official DVSA guidance.

About Upshift

Upshift is an independent directory for motorbike training schools in Edinburgh. We are not a training provider, not affiliated with the DVSA, and not a government service.

Accuracy of information

We do our best to keep listings accurate, but course details, pricing and availability change. Always confirm directly with a training school before booking. Our licence guide is a plain-English summary, not official guidance — for the current rules, see gov.uk.

No liability

Upshift is provided "as is". We're not responsible for decisions you make based on information on this site, or for the quality of training provided by any listed school.

Rankings and reviews

Where reviews exist, rankings are always based on genuine rider reviews — we don't accept payment from schools for better placement in results. Once review submission is live, fake, defamatory or abusive reviews will be removed.

Using the site

Don't use Upshift to post false, defamatory or abusive content, or to scrape or republish our content without permission.

Changes

We may update these terms as the site grows. Continued use of the site means you accept the current version.

Governing law

These terms are governed by the law of Scotland.
