About

Harley's Rider Training is a family-run school on Gorgie Road, providing CBT, A1, A2, full motorcycle licence training and Mod 1 & 2 test preparation since 1997.

Courses offered

CBT Compulsory Basic Training

Required before riding on the road on a provisional licence.

A1 Restricted licence (A1)

From age 17, on a bike up to 125cc. Needs a valid theory test certificate and CBT first — leads to a full licence with no L-plates or motorway restrictions.

A2 Restricted licence (A2)

CBT and the theory test come first, then A2 training on a restricted bike. PAYG lessons or full package courses, with gear hire (helmet, jacket, gloves) included and a free first lesson.

Full A Full licence training

Full, unrestricted motorcycle licence training.

Mod 1 & 2 Practical test preparation

Focused coaching to prepare for Module 1 and Module 2 test day.

Course pricing changes — confirm current prices directly with the school.
